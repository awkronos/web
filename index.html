The Problem

Agent data is siloed

AI agents accumulate valuable context—your preferences, interaction history, learned behaviors. Today, this data is locked in vendor-specific formats within proprietary infrastructure.

Vendor Lock-in

Switching providers means starting from scratch

No Audit Trail

Users can't review what agents have learned

No Sharing

Context can't move between different agents

Our Approach

Design principles

Portable

Switch between AI providers without losing accumulated context. Your data follows you, not your vendor.

Private

Data encrypted by default. You control who sees what through granular, explicit permissions.

Interoperable

Built on W3C standards: Solid Protocol, RDF, DIDs. Works with existing web infrastructure.

Foundation

Built on open standards

Solid Protocol

Container structure, access control, authentication

W3C DIDs

Decentralized agent identity (did:key, did:web, did:wba)

PROV-O
